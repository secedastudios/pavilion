The indie distribution trap

You spent years making your film. Then you hand it to an aggregator who takes 30%, gives you a quarterly PDF, and owns the relationship with your audience. Or you go it alone with a Vimeo link and hope for the best.

There has to be something better. There is.

One upload. Infinite screens. Your rules.

You keep ownership
Your film, your licensing terms, your audience data. Pavilion never takes rights. Change your terms or pull your content any time.
Curators come to you
Festivals, educators, and streaming entrepreneurs browse a shared, rights-aware catalog. They see only films they're allowed to use. No awkward emails. No deal sheets.
Every distribution model, one platform
Rental, purchase, subscription, ad-supported, event screening, educational, or Creative Commons. Mix and match per territory and time window. Pavilion enforces it all automatically.
Revenue flows back to you
Real-time reporting. Automatic splits. No waiting 90 days for a statement you can't verify. You see every transaction as it happens.

How it works

  1. Upload your film

    Drop in your master file. Pavilion automatically creates adaptive bitrate packages up to 4K with HLS and DASH for every device.

  2. Set your terms

    Choose which licensing models work for you. Set prices, territories, and time windows. Require approval or allow instant licensing.

  3. Curators build with your work

    Streaming platforms, festival programs, and educational libraries pull your film from the catalog. Each one gets a branded experience; you get paid.

  4. Get paid, stay informed

    Revenue splits happen automatically. You see every view, every transaction, every screen your film appears on.

Serious infrastructure for serious filmmakers

Adaptive streaming
HLS and DASH with CMAF segments. H.264, HEVC, and AV1. Works on every device, every connection.
Up to 4K
Six-rung quality ladder from 360p to 2160p. Your audience always gets the best their connection can handle.
Rights enforcement
Every video request passes through the licensing engine. No valid license, no content. Signed URLs, token-bound sessions, zero bypass.
Open source, self-hostable
Run Pavilion on your own infrastructure. No vendor lock-in. Clusters for SurrealDB, RustFS, and Qdrant scale with you.
Built with Rust
Fast, safe, and memory-efficient. Your streaming platform runs on the same technology trusted by the most performance-critical systems.

Every way to distribute, one place to manage

Transactional (TVOD)
Rent for $3.99 for 48 hours. Sell to own for $9.99. You set the price.
Subscription (SVOD)
Let curators include your film in monthly catalogs. Flat fee or revenue share based on actual watch time.
Ad-Supported (AVOD)
Free for viewers. You earn from ads with the revenue share you choose.
Events and Screenings
Flat fee or ticket split for virtual premieres, festival screenings, and community showings.
Educational
Special terms for universities, libraries, and institutions.
Creative Commons
Maximum reach when that matters more than revenue. CC BY, CC BY-SA, CC BY-NC, and more.
Hybrid and Split-Rights
Different models for different territories and time windows. Theatrical window, then PVOD, then SVOD, then AVOD. Your film, your strategy.

Why open source matters for film
