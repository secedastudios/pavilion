Content Policy

Effective: 5 April 2026

Overview

Pavilion is a platform for independent filmmakers to distribute their work. All content must comply with these standards. Violations may result in content removal and account suspension.

Content Requirements

All content uploaded to Pavilion must meet the following requirements:

  1. Copyright ownership — You must be the copyright holder or have written authorization from the copyright holder for all uploaded content.
  2. Talent clearance — All performers, actors, and recognizable individuals appearing in your content must have provided signed releases.
  3. Music and soundtrack rights — All music, sound effects, and audio must be original, licensed, or in the public domain.
  4. Location releases — Content filmed in private locations must have appropriate location releases.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited and will be immediately removed:

  • Pornographic or sexually explicit material of any kind
  • Content depicting the sexual exploitation of minors
  • Content promoting terrorism, violent extremism, or radicalization
  • Content inciting hatred based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery
  • Content that infringes on intellectual property rights of others
  • Fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading content

Enforcement

First violation
Content removed, written warning issued.
Second violation
Content removed, account suspended for 30 days.
Third violation or severe first offense
Account permanently terminated.

We also comply with DMCA takedown requests. See our DMCA policy for details.

Reporting Violations

If you believe content on Pavilion violates this policy, please report it via our DMCA and takedown request form or contact us at abuse@pavilion.film.
