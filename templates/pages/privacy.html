Privacy Policy

Effective: 5 April 2026

1. Data Controller

Pavilion operates as a data controller under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For data protection inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at the address listed at the bottom of this page.

2. Data We Collect

Account data
Name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
Profile data
Optional biography, avatar, and linked SlateHub account.
Content data
Films, metadata, and licensing terms you provide.
Usage data
Watch history, playhead positions, and platform interactions (only with your analytics consent).
Transaction data
Payment records, revenue splits, and payout history.

3. Legal Basis

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to provide our services.
  • Consent — marketing communications and analytics tracking (you can withdraw at any time).
  • Legitimate interest — security, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
  • Legal obligation — tax records, DMCA compliance.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all your personal data (export your data).
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data via your profile settings.
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data (privacy settings).
  • Portability — receive your data in machine-readable JSON format.
  • Withdraw consent — change your privacy preferences at any time.

5. Cookies

Pavilion uses only essential cookies (authentication tokens). Analytics cookies are set only with your explicit consent. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Watch session details are automatically purged after a configurable retention period. Transaction records are retained as required by applicable tax law. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed.
