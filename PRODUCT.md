# Pavilion

**One upload. Infinite screens. Your rules.**

Pavilion is an open-source film distribution and white-label OTT platform. It gives independent filmmakers real control over their work while letting curators, festivals, educators, and entrepreneurs instantly launch their own branded streaming services.

We've built it as a natural extension of SlateHub, so everything lives in one place: your talent profile, your credits, your films, and now real distribution and streaming tools.

## What Pavilion Does

You upload a film once, set your licensing and pricing rules, and then it can flow into any number of custom streaming platforms — all while you keep full ownership and control. 

Curators simply browse a shared, rights-aware catalog and pull the films they're allowed to use. No more negotiating individual deals or building a library from scratch. It's true self-distribution for filmmakers and "Netflix-in-a-box" for anyone who wants to run their own channel.

## Core Features

### For Filmmakers
- Upload once and we handle secure hosting plus all the adaptive bitrate versions you need
- Full ownership with clear, real-time revenue reporting
- Automatic payouts straight to your account
- Optional event licensing for virtual screenings, live Q&As, or ticketed windows

#### Flexible Licensing Models
Pavilion supports every major film distribution model used today so you can mix and match however you want:

- **TVOD (Transactional VOD)**: Set your own rental or purchase price — for example, $3.99 for a 48-hour rental or $9.99 to own.
- **SVOD (Subscription VOD)**: Let curators include your film in their monthly subscription catalogs. You can choose flat fees or a share based on actual views and watch time.
- **AVOD / FAST (Ad-Supported)**: Make the film free for viewers and earn from ads. You decide your revenue-share percentage.
- **Hybrid & Split-Rights**: License different territories, time windows, or rights separately (theatrical window, then PVOD, then SVOD, then AVOD, etc.).
- **Event & Screening Licensing**: Flat fee or ticket split for virtual events, festival screenings, or community showings.
- **Educational / Institutional**: Special terms for universities, libraries, or corporate use.
- **Creative Commons / Free-with-Attribution**: Open licensing when you want maximum reach and community sharing.

You set the rules once per film and Pavilion automatically enforces them everywhere.

### For Curators, Festivals & Entrepreneurs
- Browse a growing catalog of licensed films
- Launch your own fully branded streaming platform in hours (custom domain, player, playlists, analytics, and apps)
- Monetize however you like — your subscriptions, your pay-per-view, your ad revenue
- Built-in live event and co-viewing tools
- The system only shows you films you're actually allowed to use

### Technical Highlights (High-Level)
- Adaptive Bitrate streaming with HLS and DASH (using CMAF)
- Support for H.264, HEVC, and AV1 codecs
- Multiple subtitle tracks and language options
- Secure, rights-aware delivery of all video manifests

## How It Works

1. You upload your film and choose the licensing models that make sense for you.
2. Pavilion automatically creates professional adaptive bitrate packages.
3. Curators find your film in the catalog and license it (instantly or with approval).
4. Their branded streaming service starts pulling and playing your film for their audience.
5. Revenue flows back to you automatically.

## Monetization & Business Models

- Low, predictable monthly storage rent for filmmakers (based on actual usage)
- You set the pricing and terms for every film
- Curators can buy credits or go with direct revenue-share deals
- Full support for TVOD, SVOD, AVOD, hybrid, event, and educational models
- Optional small facilitation fee (5–10%) on paid transactions — you can turn this off completely on self-hosted instances

## Why Pavilion

We built Pavilion because the current options for indie filmmakers are still too limiting. Aggregators take big cuts and offer little control. White-label tools force you to source everything yourself. Pavilion sits in the middle: one upload, full control over distribution, and the ability for good curators to build real streaming businesses around your work.

It's filmmaker-first, open-source, self-hostable, and has no vendor lock-in. Plus it lives right inside SlateHub, so your existing network and profile now help power real distribution.
